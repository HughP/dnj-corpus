
<h>18 ‘Mɛ ‘ö =gwan =ya “kan ta</h>

‘Mɛ ‘ö =gwan =ya “kan ta ‘ö dho a kë kɔ ‘ö bha kö yö “kan yö =nɛ.

Blɛɛsü ‘gü, a mɛ yö ya “zɛnngdhö.

A plɛ naa,a mɛ yö ö yun bhloo minitë “sɔɔdhu ‘piʋ̈, “iin yö ‘to ‘ka “dhʋ̈ a =gwan yö ‘gbaannu. =Ya kë ‘zü =dhɛ ‘yaa yan sië, a mɛ yö yeɩ” “viëë kpö ‘në nu kë kö ya nu sɔ ö yun “dhi nu bha a nu ‘gü, kö ya nu “zɔ nu ‘to “yɛɛ. =Ya ö bo a kë “dhʋ̈ sü ‘ka kö yö ö yun nu bhloo ‘zü ‘bha wo minitë “sɔɔdhu ‘ka. =Ya ‘go mü kö ya yeɩ” “viëë nu bha a nu ‘to mü “dhɛdhɛ kpö ‘në ‘bha ‘ka, kö yö gun naa a bho ‘mü “lɛɛdhö ö ‘suaan ‘ka.

(‘Mɛ ‘ö =ya kë =sië =sië ‘ö =gwan ‘dhö kan a ta, yö nëng kë nu wo seɩ” ‘ö kë “kwi a seɩ” ‘ka, ‘ö ‘wo a dhɛ orangziö bha ‘dhö, =tanmatii nu ‘dhö, a nu bhö sü bha, kö yö mɛtii seɩ” nu “yi mü.

‘Ö kë sië “dhʋ̈, =gwan ‘yii ‘dho nu ta.
