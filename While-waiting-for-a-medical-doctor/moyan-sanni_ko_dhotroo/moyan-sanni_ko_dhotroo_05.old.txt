
<h>5 kwi ‘kun –yua</h>

Kwi ‘kun bha, “yua kpɛnngdhö ‘yaa mü. Yö kë “yua nu =në a nu =keng. Kwi ‘kun bha, yöö dede ‘ö ‘ya dhɛ ‘ö bha, yö mü, a bha da ‘gü da ‘ö wo naa =sië “gbɩɩ ‘ka dedewo ‘në ‘sɛɛnnë nu gɔ bha, a ‘ka.

=Ya kë =dhɛ ‘mɛ ‘ö “yua ‘dhö bha kö “nɛnɛ yö nu ta, ka sɔ bho a mɛ bha “pɛɛpɛdhö. =Ya kë ‘në sɛɛnnë ‘ka, -ka sɔ ‘ö bha bha, a gban bho a bha “pɛɛpɛdhö =dhɛ kɔ ‘ö ‘në ‘dhö wɔ a ‘ka ö ‘kpee ‘ka ya- ‘dhö.

A kë sü ‘ö =dhɛ kɔ bha ‘dhö, ‘yö së. ‘Ka sɔ bho  a bha ‘yö “nɛnɛ “bhʋ̈ ‘dhö =lɔɔ.

‘Yaa së kö ‘ka a kë =dhɛ kɔ ‘dhɛ ‘ö ya ‘dhö.

=Ya kë “dhʋ̈ “nɛnɛ da ‘gü =në ‘ö da “yuamɛ bha gɔ.

=Ya ‘go mü ‘mɛ “ʋʋ ‘mɛ ‘ö “nɛnɛ ‘ö a mɛ bha, yö së kö a mɛ yö “yi mü, yö “dhü bhɛ nu “yi mü.

=Ya kë ‘në sɛɛnnë ‘ka, yö “yi ‘ö =ya “tɛɛ ‘ö =ya =dhiëë bha, a mü. 

Dhɛa” ‘kpɔ: 

Yö “dhʋ̈ tɛan ‘ka =dhɛ ‘në nu ‘wa ‘dho “sukadhu pë nu bhö, =ya kë “dhʋ̈ kö bhië” ‘në nu woo gɔɔn a nu ‘gü ee ?

A yɔɔ: =sua mü. “Sukadhu pë nu bha ‘waa bhië” ‘në da ‘në nu ‘gü. “Sukadhu pë nu bhö sü bha, yö dho a nu gba “sɔn kë =në a ‘ka. A gɛn mü =dhɛ ‘në nu ‘ö ‘wo bha, a nu =miëë ‘gü dhɛ ‘ö a nu “sɔn ‘dhö gban sü ‘ka ‘gü bha, ‘yii kian =kö. A kë “dhʋ̈ sü bha ‘gü, ‘pë ‘ö bha “nɛɛ nɛɛ dhɛ ‘ö ziö sü ‘ka naa ‘gü waa pë “wɔ sü kö wa nu bhö, ‘në nu bha a nu “sɔn nu wo dho see.

=Ya ‘go mü, kö ‘në nu wo nëng kë “sukadhu pë nu bhö sü bha ‘gbɛ, bhöpë kpɛnngdhö bhö sü faan ‘yaa kë a nu ‘gü.

Yö “dhʋ̈ tɛan ‘ka =dhɛ ‘yaa së kö ‘në nu wo tɔyaan bhö, =ya kë “dhʋ̈ kö woo kë =kwan mɛ nu ‘ka ee?

A yɔɔ: ‘Aa bhoo, =sua mü. A dhɛ ya bha ziaanwo kö wa bhö. A gɛn mü =dhɛ tɔyaan bha yö mɛ kwi ‘gü dhɛ bha pë së dede =në ‘ka. A wɔn ‘gü ‘në nu bhawɔn ya bha kö wa bhö.

Yö “dhʋ̈ tɛan ‘ka =dhɛ “dhü bhɛ nu ‘ö ‘wo kë nu wo =dhɛ dhöng (seɩ”) nu, =goyaa nu ‘dhö bha ‘mɛ ‘ö ‘kpin “iin kwi ‘kun ‘dhö bha, a mɛ ‘ya ‘dho a bhö ee?

A yɔɔ: =sua mü. A dhɛ dede ziaan ya bha kö a mɛ yö “dhü bhɛ nu ‘ö ‘wo “dhʋ̈, ‘ö da ‘ka ziaanwo a “wɛɛ ‘bha nu bha bha, kö a mɛ ya bhö kö ya nu “yi nu mü.
