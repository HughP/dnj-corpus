
<h>2 “Yua ‘ö mɛ kë mɛ ga yö ‘ko “dhiʋ̈ dhɛ nu ‘gü (=Gla “kpɛa)</h>

“Yua ‘ö mɛ kë mɛ ga yö ‘ko “dhiʋ̈ dhɛ -nu ‘gü bha, ‘mɛ ‘ö ‘yii a kë së, yö mɔɔ a bha ‘ö mɛ “pɛɛn do ga a bha.

‘Mɛ ‘ö ‘bha ga =dhɛ ü ga yö ‘ko “dhiʋ̈ dhɛ nu wo bhɔ wɛɛndhö, ‘ö “yi nu ‘wo kan nu wo “slʋ̈ʋ̈dhö, ‘bha ‘dho ü de sëë bho ‘zü, a “dhɛ “gbɩɩ ‘ö ‘dhö a ga ‘ö ya:

Mlü “flɩɩ bha, yɛɛn” “piʋ̈ kö bhii ‘dho yi ‘gü, ‘yö ‘ü a “sang ‘në yan yiisiö dan. Pë bhö ‘ka “sang =në ‘ö ‘kwa wɩ a ‘ka. ‘Bha a dan ‘yö ‘ü dɔ “yëë ( kö kwa pö ‘ü “yi sɛa=loo bha). “Yi bha yö ‘to bha “dhʋ̈ kö dhɛ yö =kpaɔ. 

‘Bha bö dhia” “piʋ̈ kö ‘bhii pë ‘bha bhö =kö, ‘yö ‘ü “siʋ̈ ya. “Yi ‘dhɛ ‘ö wɔ mlü “flɩɩ bha ‘ö kë “yi blɛɛsü ‘ka bha, ‘yö ‘ü a bho a bha, ‘ü “yi dee “wɛɛ ‘në ‘bha =loo pë ‘në ‘bha ‘‘gü, ‘ü dɔ “siʋ̈ ta ‘ü mlü ga bha, a =loo ‘gü. ‘Bha ga =dhɛ yöö ya “tɛɛ sü bha bhoo, ‘yö ‘ü bho “siʋ̈ ta, ‘yö “yi =plɛ naa bha ‘ü bho a bha ‘zü, ‘yö ‘ü “yi “wɛɛ ‘bha =loo bha, ‘ü dɔ “siʋ̈ ta. Yaa pö yöö ya “tɛɛ sü bha, ‘yö ‘ü a bho ‘mü, ‘ü “yi ‘dhɛ bha, a bho bha. Kö ya kë “yi yaaga naa ‘ka.

‘Yö ‘ü a dee “wɛɛ ‘bha =loo pë do bha ‘gü ‘ü mlü bha a =loo =bhaa. Yaa pö yöö “tɛɛ, ‘yö ‘ü- bho ‘mü, ‘yö “yi bha ‘ü bho bha, ‘ü a dee ‘bha= loo bha, ‘ü dɔ “siʋ̈ ta. Yöö “tɛɛ, ‘yö ‘ü- bho ‘mü, ‘ü “yi ‘dhɛ bha a bho bha.=Ya kë “dhʋ̈ kö “yi ‘ö ‘ü a ziö mlü ga bha, a bha “dhia “piʋ̈ bha, ü a ziö a bha ‘ko ta yiisiö. A ‘dhɛ ‘ö wɔ a bha ‘ö dhɛ yö =kpaɔ bha, ya da mü kö ya kë “sɔɔdhu. =Ya ‘go mü ‘yö ‘ü mlü ‘ö ya dhëë “kan bha ‘ü bhö, ‘ü =gban bhö. ‘Bha ü bo a bhö sü ‘ka, kö ‘bhaa bhii pë “wɛɛ ‘bha bhö a ziaan ‘gü “dhɛdhɛ yiisiö “piʋ̈. 

A “sunma yö kë =dhɛ kö ‘ü a bhö “dhɛdhɛ “saaga (8) -naa ‘ka dhia” “piʋ̈, kö bhii ü “yan ‘to ‘yënng ya mɛ gɔ =zinng ‘gü “tʋ̈ng ‘ö kë=mingdi ‘ka bha,=në a gɔ kö ‘ü gun naa pë bhö.

(Ü dho a kë “dhʋ̈ dhɛkpaɔyi kɔng =plɛ ö ga “saaga “piʋ̈. ‘Waa wlö dhɛkpaɔyi “wɛɛ ‘bha ta tongtongdhö. ‘Bha kë “dhʋ̈ kö ‘bha “kian zë. Kö bhii a zü dee bho.

A mlü ga “yi “nangglüü nangglüü ‘ö yöö da ü ga yö ‘ko “dhiʋ̈ dhɛ nu ‘gü bha, =në yöö dhii nu ‘ö ‘wo mü, ‘ö ‘wo ü ga ‘gü dhɛ nu bhɔ sië bha, a nu bho ‘mü.

Dhɛkpaɔyi kɔng =plɛ ö ga “saaga bha kö ‘ü nu yö ‘ko “dhiʋ̈ kö ‘bha ü de dha dedewo. Ü dho kë “klʋ̈ʋ̈klʋ̈.
