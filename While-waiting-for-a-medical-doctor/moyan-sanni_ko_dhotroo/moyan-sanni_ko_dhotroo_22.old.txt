
<h>22 Dhɛ ‘ö “kwi naa pë böö ‘ö ‘wo wo “sɔn zu a ‘ka ‘yaa bha</h>

=Ya kë =dhɛ pë böö ‘ö ‘wo ya “sɔn zu ‘ka pë “dhiʋ̈ bha, ‘yaa dhɛ ‘ö bha “iin ‘mɛ ‘ö gɔ, a mɛ yö =wee bi ‘në sɛɛnnë waa ‘seng -bi sɛɛnnë kaan “kwëë ya ya “sɔn zu ‘ka pë ta. “Kɛɛ yö “sɔn zu ‘ka pë bha, a dhɛ ‘ö yöö ziö ö “sɔn bha bha, a =tɔɔ =kö blɛɛsü “yi ‘ka kö pë bi bha, yö ‘ma ‘gü së ‘ka. =Ya ‘go mü kö ya ziö ö “sɔn bha. Yö dho a “sɔn ‘zu së ‘ka dedewo.

‘Mɛ ‘ö ‘seng‘yaa a gɔ, yö mɔɔ a bha ‘ö kë =wee bi doseng ‘ka.
