
<h>10 ‘Ö ü “piʋ̈ ‘ü kë ü faan ‘gü kpɛɛwo: “nimlɔɔ blɛɛsü</h>

  ‘Mɛ ‘ö “piʋ̈ ‘ö kë ö faan ‘gü “tʋ̈ng =gban ‘gü,‘ö “piʋ̈ “gʋng ‘ya ‘dho ö kë =plɛplɛ, dhia” “piʋ̈ =gban “pɛpɛ ‘gü, a mɛ ya bö kö ‘yii pë “wɛɛ ‘bha bhö =kö, “yi dede ‘ö ya, a sandhe, “iin ‘zü a zuɛɛbhɛ, “kwi yö ö bha pë dhɛ ‘dhiitrë, a mɛ ya do mü. Ya ö bo a kë “dhʋ̈ sü ‘ka, ‘yö yöö pë bhö, ‘yö gun a bhö naa =tɔn. “Kɛɛ a mɛ yö ö ‘gü dɔ “gbɩɩ ‘ya ‘dho pö ‘ö dhɛkpaɔyi nu da a “yi bha a mü sü =zinng ‘gü. A kë “dhʋ̈ sü bha ‘yaa së.
  
  ‘Wɔn ‘ö bha, ‘mɛ ‘ö ya =bhlëë ‘gü dedewo, kö a mɛ =ya ‘wɔn së kë ö de dhɛ.
