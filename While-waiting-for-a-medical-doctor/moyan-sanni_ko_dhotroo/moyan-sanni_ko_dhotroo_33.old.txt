
<h>33 “Gblü ziö</h>

Ka “gblü ‘dhö ziö sië:

• -ka -bhöpë ‘ö “Kwi ‘dhö- -dhɛ karɔtë bha, -a -kpa “wɛɛwɛ -së ‘ka. =Ya ma, -a “yi ‘dhö oo, -a karɔtë bha ‘dhö oo, -ka =wee ‘në ‘bha kë- -bha kö ‘ka -a “yi bha mü, ‘ka- karɔtë -bhö.
• 
• -Ka gwɛ bhɛ ‘ö go ö -dhü ‘gü -dee ‘gü bha, -a ‘sü. -Kaa “kɛɛ bho- -	bha, kö ‘ka- -da “yi ‘ö “sukadhu ‘dhö- -bha, -a =bhaa. =Ya ‘ma- -bha =së ‘ka, kö ‘ka- mü.
• 
• -Ka glɔɔ -pëkë -sü (tan -sü) -bhö.
