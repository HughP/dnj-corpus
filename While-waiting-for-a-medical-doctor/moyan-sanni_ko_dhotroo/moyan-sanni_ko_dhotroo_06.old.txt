
<h>6 ‘Yuöö “glɛɛn =ya =nëng ü bhɛa” (2)</h>

‘Ü pë bhö sië kö ‘yuöö “glɛɛn yö =nëng ü bhɛa”, ‘yuöö ‘ö go ‘tɛɛ dede ‘ö gü bha, ü =wee pa ‘ka kɔngga “iin ‘zü ‘tɛa ‘gü pa ‘ka kɔngga bha, a ma ‘tɛɛ dede bha a “tʋ “dhiʋ̈ pinngpinngdhö. Yö dho ‘to ‘ka “dhʋ̈ ‘ö ‘yuöö “glɛɛn bha ‘ö =lɔɔ, ‘ö go ü bhɛa”, ‘ö kë ü dhasü ‘ka.
