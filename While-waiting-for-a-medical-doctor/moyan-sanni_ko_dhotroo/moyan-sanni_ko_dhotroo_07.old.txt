
<h>7 Bhangglöö(bandhe), =bɛdhɛ “gbɩɩ mü</h>

Bhangglöö bhɛ ‘ö ya pëkë (tan) bha, yö mɛ gba pëbhee ‘wɛɛ ‘bha nu bhö dhɔ ‘ka.

‘Mɛ nu ‘ö wü waa tɔyaan nu bha a nu bhö sü ‘ö nu bha “gbɩɩ, waa pö woo a nu bhö, wo mɔɔ a bha ‘ö ‘wo nu bhö bhangglöö pëkë sü ‘ka.

Bhië” ‘në =bɛdhɛ “gbɩɩ bha. A mɛ ya ‘gü dan.
