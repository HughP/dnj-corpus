
<h>17 ‘Mɛ ‘ö ‘gu =ya ma bha</h>

‘Mɛ ‘ö ‘gu =ya ‘ma bha (‘ö ‘yaa ö gboo sië) ‘ö dho a kë kɔ ‘ö bha yö =nɛ.

A mɛ yö yabha kpö do ‘sü ya kaan kaan. =Ya ö bo ko ya =loo “yi “vɛdhɛ yan do “iin =plɛ ‘ö =ya “sukadhu da a bha a =bhaa, ya dɔ “siʋ̈ʋ̈ kö yö “tɛɛ “tʋ̈ng kpö ‘në sɛɛnnë ‘ka (minitë kɔng do “iin kɔngdo ö ga “sɔɔdhu “piʋ̈). =Ya bo “tɛɛ sü ‘ka, =ya =dhiëë, dhia” “piʋ̈, ‘mɛ bha kö ‘yii pë ‘bha bhö =kö, kö ya “vɛdhɛ yan do mü. Yɛɛn” “piʋ̈ kö yöö ‘dho yi ‘gü, kö ya do mü. 

Kö ya kë “dhʋ̈ sü =bhlëë ya, a ‘gu ta yö dho lɔɔ 

“Iin ‘zü ‘mɛ ‘ö a ‘gu =ya ‘ma bha, a mɛ yö mɛtii seɩ” bhɛ do “iin =plɛ ‘sü ya “yi bho ya =loo “yi ‘ö ‘yaa “wɔwɔsü “iin ‘ö ‘yaa sɛa bha ( ‘kwa pö “dhʋ̈ kö kwa a pö “yi ‘kpɔng kpɔng), a =bhaa. Ya kaan ‘ka së ‘ka. =Ya ö bo kö ya mü. “Kɛɛ ‘ö mü sië kö yö “ta ‘sü sië- ‘ka “lɛɛdhö, kë “dhʋ̈ kö yö da a kwi ‘gü së ‘ka, ya ‘gu lɔɔ.
