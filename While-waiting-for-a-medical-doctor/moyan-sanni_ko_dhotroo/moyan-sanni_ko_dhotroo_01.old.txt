
<h>1) ‘Yuöö “glɛɛn ya =nëng mɛ bhɛa”</h>


  =Ya kë =dhɛ ‘yuöö “glɛɛn “iin pë “wɛɛ ‘bha =ya =nëng mɛ ‘bha bhɛa” kö a bho mü kɔ “slë ‘yaa ‘dhö, kö a mɛ ‘yaa “tɛɛ dhɛ yö sië së ‘ka, ‘wɔn ‘ö dhɛ ‘dhö a bha kö ‘ü a kë a mɛ gɔ yö =nɛ:
  
  Blɛɛsü ‘gü, bhö dɔ a mɛ =taama =dhɛ kɔ ‘ö ü “yan ‘dhö -a -bha ‘sëëdhɛ -ta ya- ‘dhö. Ü gbe nu bɔ a gɔ; ü kɔdhɛ da “kʋʋ.
  
A =plɛ naa ’gü, ü kɔdhɛ bha a =nëng a ‘gu ta a bugɔ gɔ “dhuʋ̈ dhɛ waa “sɩga nu =zinngü. Ü kɔ ‘ya ‘dho bɔ a “sɩga ta.

  A yaaga naa ‘gü,a ‘gu bha a bhloo “gbɩɩ ‘ka ‘ko ta ‘gbɛ ‘ka kö “tɛɛ yö ‘go a gɔ “sia =yën -yö da. “Kɛɛ bhöö bhloo “vaandhö ‘ko ta ‘gbɛ ‘ka. ‘Bha a kë “dhʋ̈ “tɛɛ yö dho ‘go a tong ‘gü ‘ö ‘pë ‘ö bhɛa” bha a bho mü. Ü mɔɔ a bha ‘ü a kë a gɔ “dhʋ̈ pinngpinngdhö “ni kö ‘yaa ‘go sië ‘mü.
  
￹￹=Ya kë ‘zü =dhɛ ‘mɛ ‘ö pë ‘ö =nëng a bhɛa” bha faan ya kë a bha =ya ziö- ‘ka ü ta, kö “yan së ‘yaa a gɔɔ ‘zü, a ‘waannu =dhɛ kɔ ‘ö ya a ‘dhö “vaandhö:

A wɔ ö ko ta. Bhö dɔ a ta =dhɛ kɔ ‘ö ya a ‘dhö. Ü kɔ =plɛ bha a wɔ ‘ko –ta, a bugɔ waa “sɩga =zinng ‘gü. =Nëng a ‘gu bha ta “gbɩɩ ‘ka “kɛɛ “vaandhö wɔn ‘ka kö “tɩng yö da. A kë “dhʋ̈ ‘ko ta ‘gbɛ ‘ka “ni kö ‘pë bha ‘yaa ‘go sië ‘mü.
