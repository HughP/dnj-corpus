
<h>26 Dhioo nu ‘ö ‘ö ‘wo –së kwa –bha ‘ka</h>

Blɛɛsü ‘gü:

‘wëëga ‘në ‘bha =ya kë ‘mɛ ‘ö a gɔ, ‘ö mɛ “piʋ̈ =dhɛ ‘ö ö bha ‘në ta ‘kun kö yö kë ö faan ‘gü së ‘ka “tʋ̈ng =gban ‘gü, a mɛ ‘ya ‘dho we “nɛɛnɛɛ ‘dhɔ ö bha ‘në bha dhɛ. 

A=plɛ naa: 

a mɛ yö tɔyaan nu, “gwɛɛ nu, “nɔnɔ sɛa “iin =kplü ‘kun sü, “iin “dhü bhɛ nu ‘ö ‘wo kë nu wo dhöng (seɩ”), =tanmatii nu, ze nu, “kpako nu, bhangglöö (bandhe) nu, =vokaa nu, “manggloo nu, glɔɔ “nɛɛnɛɛ nu ‘ö da a ‘ka a “wɛɛ ‘bha nu bha bha, a mɛ ya ‘dhɔ ö bha ‘në dhɛ “tʋ̈ng ‘gbɛ dhɛ ‘gü.

A yaaga naa: 

dhebë ‘ö ‘në dee ‘ö gɔ “yɔn bha, ‘pë nu ‘ö ‘kwa go a nu ‘tɔ ma bha, a dhebë ‘ö “dhʋ̈ bha, ya nu bhö “tʋ̈ng ‘gbɛ dhɛ ‘gü, kë “dhʋ̈ kö yö da a ‘yɔn ‘gü së ‘ka, kö yö kaan a “yɔn “yi ‘ka, kö ‘në yaa mü,yö kë ö faan ‘gü së dede ‘ka.

A yiisiö naa: 

baa ‘ö kë bhö baa ‘ka bha, a “dhɛ bha, kwa nëng kë a bhö sü bha. Bhöpë së dede mü. Yö mɛ da mɛ faan ‘gü. Kwa mɔɔ a bha ‘ö ‘kwa a =flügüdhe kë ‘pö.=Ya ‘go mü, “wɩsɩ “dhɛ nu, ze “dhɛ nu, kplɔɔ” “dhɛ nu waa “kwi a ze “dhɛ nu ‘wo bha, kwa mɔɔ a nu bha ‘ö ‘kwa a nu “kpɛa kpɔ ‘ö ‘kwa nu bi bho ‘dodo ‘ka, ‘ö ‘kwa nu =kaan –dee ‘në nu bha “bhaa “yi ‘ka. Yö ‘në nu da faan ‘gü.
