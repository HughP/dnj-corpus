
<h>4 Köng bhɛ “sɛɛ bha =bɛdhɛ “gɩɩ mü</h>

Dhebë nu ‘ö ‘wo ‘gbiöö ‘ö nu gɛn =ya bhla, a nu bha=bɛdhɛ do =nɛ:

Köng (“Maa “sɛ ‘gü mɛ nu wo woo pë dhɛ kpɛa) bhɛ “sɛɛ ‘ö bha, =bɛdhɛ së dede mü dhebë nu ‘ö ‘wo ‘gbiöö ‘ö nu gɛn =ya bhla, a nu bha ‘ka. ‘Wo dho a kë kɔ ‘ö bha, yö =nɛ: köng bhɛ “sɛɛ ‘ö bha, waa kun =kwɛɛ tiantiandhe bho, “iin ‘zü a ‘dhɛ ‘ö dho =mɔɔ a nu =kwɛɛ tiantiandhe ‘ka, waa ‘sü, ‘yo ‘wo a kpa së ‘ka, a “yi ‘ö bha, ‘yö ‘wo a “vɛdhɛ yan do “iin =plɛ mü.
