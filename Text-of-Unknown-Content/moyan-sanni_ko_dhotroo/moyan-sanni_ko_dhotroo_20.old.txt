
<h>20 Dɔn “kpɛa =tɔɔ =bɛdhɛ</h>

‘Mɛ ‘ö dɔn “kpɛa ‘dhö bha, a mɛ =ya “yi dɔ “siʋ̈ʋ̈, =ya “tɛɛ dedewo së ‘ka, ‘yö a mɛ ‘dhö a =loo “gbʋnggbʋ ‘gü ‘ö ö gɔ dun a ta kö a mɛ =ya sɔ ‘ma ö gɔ waa “gbʋnggbʋ =gban bha. Yaa kë “dhʋ̈ ‘yö a “dhuu bha ‘ö a gan ö “dhi waa ö yun ‘gü. ‘Yö a kë “dhʋ̈, ‘ko ta ‘gbɛ ‘ka dhɛkpaɔyi bha.

‘Mɛ ‘ö a yun ‘gü =ya ta, a mɛ =ya kë =dhɛ kɔ do ‘ö ‘go sië a ma ‘ka, dɔn “kpɛa bha ‘ka bha ‘dhö.
