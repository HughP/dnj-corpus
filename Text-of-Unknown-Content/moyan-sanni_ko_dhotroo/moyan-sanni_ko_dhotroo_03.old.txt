
<h>3 Dhebë ‘ö ‘gbiöö ‘ö dho kë kɔ ‘ö bha kö yö ö ‘në ‘kpɔ yi dɔ</h>

Kɔ ‘ö dho, ö ‘në ‘kpɔ “tʋ̈ng dɔ ‘ka yö nɛ:

Dhebë bha, ö kɔ “zu kɔ to =taama sü ‘ö a kë, “iin ‘ö bɔ ‘ka dhebë nu bɔ dhɛ ‘gü bha, ‘ö sü dhɛkpaɔyi to =taama sü bha bha, yö “su yaaga bho “dhiʋ̈, “iin “kan- “dhiʋ̈. =Ya ‘go mü kö yö dhɛkpaɔyi “saɔplɛ (7) zuö a ta.

A “sunman yö kë =dhɛ kö a bha kɔ “zu sü dhɛkpaɔyi to =taama sü yö kë “su ‘ö kë “kwi a “su ‘ka, ‘ö kë zangvie ‘ka bha, a dhɛkpaɔyi kɔngdo (10) ‘ka, kö kwaa dhong =ni: zangvie dhɛkpaɔyi kɔngdo, =wa “su yaaga “kan “dhiʋ̈ kö yö dho bɛ “su ‘dhɛ ‘ö ‘wo dhɛ Ɔkötɔbrë “kwi wo ‘gü bha, a dhɛkpaɔyi kaɔng do –bha. =Ya kë “dhʋ̈, =wa dhɛkpaɔyi “saɔplɛ da a ta, kö ya kë Ɔkötɔbrë dhɛkpaɔyi kɔngdo ö ga “saɔplɛ. Kö “tʋ̈ng ‘ö bha a zü dhɛ bha yö dho ‘në ‘kpɔ ‘ka. Kö dhɛkpaɔyi kɔng do ö ga “saɔdo, ö ga “saɔplɛ, ö ga “saaga waa ö ga “suɩsiö bha, yö dho ‘në ‘kpɔ a do ‘ka.

=Ya kë “dhʋ̈, kö yö së kö ‘mɛ ‘ö a “piʋ̈ ‘ö ö ‘në ‘kpɔ yi dɔ, yö ö “tʋ̈ng kë dhɛkpaɔyi tomaasü ‘ö “su yö a ‘ka bha, a ‘gü.
