
<h>25 ‘Mɛ ‘ö yi ‘yaa da sië a “yan –bha</h>

‘Mɛ ‘ö “dhʋ̈ bha, yɛɛn” “piʋ̈, a mɛ yö ö ‘zu “yi ‘wɔɔ wɔɔ ‘ka. ‘Kwa pö “dhʋ̈ kö kwa pö “yi ‘ö ‘yaa “wɔsü “iin ‘ö ‘yaa sɛa. =Ya ‘go mü, a mɛ yö “nɔnɔ “wɔwɔsü “vɛdhɛ yan do mü kö yö gun‘waannu naa. =Ya ‘go ‘bha wo ‘mü, ‘ö sü ‘yënng ya mɛ gɔ =zinng ‘gü “tʋ̈ng bha ‘ö dho ‘ö yö a ‘ka yɛɛn” “piʋ̈ bha, a mɛ ‘ya ‘dho “kaflɩɩ “iin ‘pë ‘ö ‘wo ‘te kë bha, a ‘bha mü tongtongdhö.
