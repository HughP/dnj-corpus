
<h>13 “Sɔn kë =bɛdhɛ</h>

  ‘Mɛ ‘ö ü “sɔn ‘dhö ü kë sië, =ya kë =dhɛ ü “sɔng ga ‘ö ü kë sië bha a yɛ ga ‘dhö ‘gü, ‘bha ‘dho ü de sëë bho, =bɛdhɛ ‘ö ya a kë.
  
  Ü “sɔnga bha ‘ö ü kë sië “tʋ̈ng dede ‘ö ‘ka bha, ‘bha ‘dho ‘kɔ =taama kë dhɛ ‘gü, yee” “viëë ‘në ‘bha yö kë ü gɔ. ‘Bha ‘kɔ =taama bha kë, ü ‘gü dɔ “gbɩɩ kö ‘ü ü dega gbo ‘ö bha, ‘ü sɛɛnnë ‘bha ‘sü “dhü ‘në “zɔ “dhiʋ̈ kö ‘ü a ya ‘gü, ‘ü yee” “viëë bha a ya ta. Ü mɔɔ a bha ‘ö ‘ü kë “dhʋ̈ ‘ko ta =plɛ “iin yaaga dhɛkpaɔyi do bha ‘ka. Kö ü gbo ‘ü ya a ‘gü bha, a =fii ‘kö bha yö da ü =miëë bha a “piʋ̈, kö ü “sɔn bha yö dɔ dinngdhö, kö ya kë a bo sü =në a ‘ka bha. ‘yii ‘dho ü kë ‘zü tongtongdhö ‘ö to ‘ka “dhʋ̈ kwɛ ‘gbɛ dede “piʋ̈.
  
  A ‘gü ma ‘ka =dhɛ ü dega gbo mü. A kë ‘“dhʋ̈ sü ‘gü, a yënng ‘ya ‘dho ü kë. Mɛ ‘yaa yënng kë mɛ dha pë gɔ.
